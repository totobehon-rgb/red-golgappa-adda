MegaBox — Autonomous AI Mind
Not a chatbot. MegaBox is a fully local autonomous AI with a real-time emotional state, camera-based vision, face recognition, voice interaction, and a live neural dashboard. It thinks, observes, remembers, and forms genuine opinions. Routes between Llama3, Qwen2.5-Coder and LLaVA:7b depending on context. Knows who it's talking to via face recognition, reacts when you disappear from camera, and speaks with emotion-matched animations. Zero cloud dependency — everything runs on your machine.

◈Emotional self-state (mood, energy, curiosity, focus)

◈Face recognition — knows who is in frame
◈Emotion-aware character animations on dashboard
◈Voice I/O with smart name recognition
◈Internal thinking system with self-reflection
◈Identity defense — reacts when called a chatbot
◈Real-time neural interface dashboard
